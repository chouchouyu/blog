		1.   encumber
		本意指“造成负担”(to burden)，比喻意指“阻碍”(to impede or hamper the function or activity of )
		Chinese students are encumbered by the heavy load of homework and the expectation of their parents.
		现在的学生不仅课业负担沉重，家长的期望也令他们感到压力重大。

		2.   foil/stymie/thwart
		这一组词指“阻碍或挫败工作、目标、计划的完成”
		to oppose or defeat the efforts, goals or plans.
		The company hopes the device can help foil/stymie/thwart thieves.
		该公司希望这种装置能让小偷无法得逞。
		My busy schedule and a lack of seed money foiled /stymied/thwarted the project.
		我太忙，又没有弄到启动资金，所以计划就歇菜了。

		3.   hamper/handicap/hinder/impede
		这一组词都指“阻碍”(to check or obstruct the progress of )
		Governments issued an avalanche of laws and regulations banning the use of human embryonic stem cells in scientific research. These restrictions could hamper /handicap/ hinder /impede advances in medical research.

		这些限制可能会阻碍医学研究的进步。
		3G mobile phones ‘expensive and bulky handsets and short battery life hamper/handicap/hinder/impede sales.
		3G手机昂贵又笨重的机身和极短的电池寿命影响了销售。
		Anti-dumping laws are increasingly used by Western countries to safeguard the feeble domestic produces. They have hampered/handicapped/hindered/impeded competition and free trade.
		反倾销法在实践中已越来越多地被西方国家用于保护实力弱的国内厂商。它们已经阻碍了竞争与自由贸易。

		4.   retard
		本意指“使减慢速度”(to cause to move slowly),也可以指“阻碍”(to impede)
		The Microsoft always claims that the prevalent piracy in China has retarded the growth in Microsoft’s software sales.
		Instead of retarding the company’s growth in China, piracy has actually expanded Microsoft’s user base.
		微软总是说中国泛滥的盗版阻碍了微软软件销售额的增长。盗版非但没有阻碍微软在中国的发展，反而在实际上扩大了该公司的用户群。
		5.   smother/stifle
		本意指 “使窒息“(to suffocate),比喻意指”压制、约束“(to suppress, curb)
		Microsoft’s monopoly has smothered/stifled competition and innovation in the computer industry.
		微软的垄断地位阻碍了计算机产业中的竞争与创新。
		But some people are concerned that over-regulation could smother/stifle the recovery of the U.S economy.

		但是，有些人担心，管理过严可能会阻碍美国经济的恢复。
